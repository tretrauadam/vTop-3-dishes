My Top Three vietnamese dishes

get drooling

Banh Xeo

Delicious pancakes rich with a variety of flavours, each region has its own style. banh meaning batter and xeo is the sound of frying these yummy dishes are famous for late night snacking and smashing that daily calorie limit

Bun Bo hue

A hearty bowl of goodness, this 1 dollar bowl packs a massive punch. thick rice noodles with with pork and spices it is abolutely delicious. As the name implies, it comes from the old capital of Hue. It can be a bit on the spicy side, so do avoid on first dates

Save some space for this badboy

Bun Cha

Fresh midday Bun cha with side spring rolls and a cold coke with ice... oh it doesn't get better than that. In the words of st patron Bourdain it's fucking good. grill porked in a sweet papaya sauce, thin rice noodles and garlic. so simple yet delicious.
